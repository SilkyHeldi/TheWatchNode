## 1. Introduction
Welcome to **TheWatchNode** (https://thewatchnode.com). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

As a static website, TheWatchNode does not have user accounts, login systems, or databases. We do not collect personal information like your name, address, or phone number.

- Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to this site or other sites on the Internet.

## 4. Log Files
Like most websites, we may use log files. These files merely log visitors to the site—usually a standard procedure for hosting companies and hosting services' analytics (GitHub Pages). The information inside the log files includes IP addresses, browser type, and time stamps. This information is not linked to anything that is personally identifiable.
